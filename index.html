Teach Herbs Clean and Correctly

A master cabinet includes preparation knowledge, not just names.

Infusion

Best for leaves and flowers. Pour hot water, cover, steep, strain.

Decoction

Best for roots, barks, seeds, and tougher plant material. Simmer low and slow.

Tincture

Concentrated extract. Stronger, shelf-stable, and needs careful labeling.

Salve

Oil-infused herbs thickened with wax or butter for topical use.

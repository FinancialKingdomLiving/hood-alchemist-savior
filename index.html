Digital Grow Guide + Moon Planting System

Built for Cartersville, Georgia growers: use the season, the plant part, and moon phase together.

Spring Push

  • Start hardy herbs.
  • Plant warm-season herbs after frost.
  • Waxing moon: leaves, flowers, fruits.

Summer Strength

  • Mulch and water deep.
  • Use afternoon shade for tender herbs.
  • Harvest leaves before flowering.

Fall Reset

  • Great for perennials and roots.
  • Waning moon: roots and soil work.
  • Build soil with compost.

Winter Planning

  • Plan beds and order seeds.
  • Protect tender perennials.
  • Stratify seeds needing cold.

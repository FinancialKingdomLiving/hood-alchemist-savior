Digital Grow Guide + Moon Planting System

Built for Cartersville, Georgia growers: use the season, the plant part, and the moon phase together. Above-ground crops traditionally favor the waxing moon; roots and underground crops traditionally favor the waning moon.

Spring Push

  • Start hardy herbs and cool-season greens early.
  • Plant warm-season herbs after frost risk passes.
  • Waxing moon: leaves, flowers, fruits.

Summer Strength

  • Use mulch, deep watering, and afternoon shade for tender herbs.
  • Harvest leaves before flowering for strong oils.
  • Watch humidity, mildew, aphids, and mites.

Fall Reset

  • Great window for perennials, roots, and cool-season herbs.
  • Waning moon: roots, bulbs, transplant recovery.
  • Build soil with compost and leaf mulch.

Winter Planning

  • Plan beds, order seeds, stratify seeds that need cold.
  • Protect tender perennials from hard cold snaps.
  • Review harvest notes and update formulas.
